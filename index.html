ABOUT US

Brief History

In 1983, when integrators were lording over the livestock market in Southern Tagalog, a traditional livestock and poultry raising region, fifteen (15) entrepreneurs saw the opportunity to provide a local alternative. Being livestock producers themselves, they pooled their resources and put up a feedmill plant geared to produce high quality and nutritionally balanced rations for use in their own farms. Thus in April 1983, Lipa Agricultural Development Corp. (LADECO) was established.

It did not take long for other farm owners – hog, cattle and poultry producers to likewise benefit from the high quality by reasonably – price feeds. Institutional buyers and a growing network of distributors and dealers in the Southern Tagalog region help the company to take off from producing more and introduce additional product lines.

Over the years, LADECO has weathered and survived not only economic downturns but fluctuations in market demand. A key factor to its endurance as a leading commercial feed producer is its averring commitment to quality of feeds and customer service.

Not only do LADECO feeds adhere to the strictest monitoring of all inputs and raw materials, its rations are carefully formulated and farm-tested. LADECO believes in investing in equipment and testing of new products in a continued effort to improve feed quality, not only to adapt to the changing needs of the market but also to benefit from development in animal nutrition research.
